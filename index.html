LogBud Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026

Developer: LogBud Developer

1. Information We Collect

LogBud collects information you enter into the app, including:

  • Technician details (name, certification number, qualifications)
  • NDT logbook entries (hours, job descriptions, inspection records)
  • Sign-off profiles and digital signatures
  • Account credentials (email address and password) used to access cloud features

The app does not automatically collect analytics, location data, or advertising identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • Display and manage your NDT hour logs inside the app.
  • Sync your data securely to the cloud so you can access it across devices.
  • Authenticate your account and keep your data private to you.
  • Generate PDFs and summaries that you can export or share yourself.

3. Cloud Storage and Third-Party Services

LogBud uses Supabase as its cloud backend to store and sync your data securely. Supabase is a third-party service and your data is stored on servers hosted by Supabase, Inc. Data may be stored on servers located in the United States or other regions.

Supabase uses industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS) and at rest. You can review Supabase's privacy practices at supabase.com/privacy.

No other third parties receive your data unless you manually export and share files yourself.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or any unrelated third parties. Your data is only transmitted to Supabase for cloud sync and account management as described above.

5. Security

All data transmitted between the app and our cloud backend is encrypted using TLS. Your account is protected by the authentication system provided by Supabase. We recommend using a strong, unique password for your LogBud account.

Local data stored on your device resides in the app file system. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data.

6. Your Choices and Data Deletion

  • You can edit or delete individual logbook entries within the app at any time.
  • You can remove all local data by uninstalling the app.
  • To request deletion of your cloud account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

LogBud is intended for professional and training use by adults in the NDT industry. The app is not directed to children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. The effective date above will be updated when changes are made.

9. Contact

Questions or requests? Email Development@logbudsoftware.com.
